Our Story

Fifteen years of weaving tradition into every thread. From the looms of Andhra Pradesh to your doorstep — this is Butta Bomma.

Born from a Love of
Traditional Craft

Butta Bomma was founded in 2010 with a simple vision — to bring authentic, handwoven Indian textiles to women across the world who love and cherish their cultural roots.

The name "Butta Bomma" — meaning doll in a dotted saree — is a nod to the intricate butta (motif) work that defines our signature collection. Each piece in our store is sourced directly from master weavers in Dharmavaram, Pochampally, Uppada, and Varanasi.

Today, we serve over 10,000 customers across the US, Canada, UK, and Australia — bringing the finest Indian craftsmanship to the global diaspora.
